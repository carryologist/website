Coder Workspaces: Perfect for Agentic AI

Enterprise AI that's secure, governed, and scalable.

Essential Guardrails

Ensure AI doesn't create chaos. Role-based access controls govern exactly what a workspace (and AI agent) can access. Secrets management prevents credential leaks, while resource quotas prevent runaway usage.

Right-Sized Infrastructure

Provision secure, consistent environments for AI agents instantly. Tailor infrastructure to task requirements with infrastructure-as-code, controlling resources as AI models and usage patterns evolve.

Fully Isolated Workspaces

Give AI a sandbox to work in - real environments with the right tools, but isolated so if they break something, it doesn't affect production. Workspaces can be rebuilt in minutes for safe experimentation.
